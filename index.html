About Us

RK Agri Mart is a dedicated farm produce supply company based in Karnataka, focused on delivering fresh and nutritious Ragi (Finger Millet), fruits, and vegetables directly from local farmers. Our mission is to bridge the gap between farmers and communities by ensuring fair prices for growers and high-quality, affordable produce for our customers. We proudly support healthy eating habits and sustainable agriculture through our wholesale and export services.

Our Products

Fresh, Local, and Nutritious

At RK Agri Mart, we take pride in offering a carefully curated range of farm-fresh products sourced directly from local farmers. From our nutrient-rich Ragi to seasonal fruits and vegetables, every item is selected for its quality, freshness, and health benefits. Whether you're a family, a hotel, or an event organizer, we provide wholesome produce at competitive wholesale prices.

Ragi (Finger Millet)

Our flagship product, Ragi is a powerhouse of nutrients and antioxidants. Sourced directly from trusted local farmers, we ensure top-notch quality and freshness. Available in: 1Kg | 5Kg | 10Kg pocket. Options: Whole millets & Ragi powder

Vegetables

We offer a wide range of fresh, seasonal vegetables grown by local farmers. Ideal for hotels, caterers, and family functions at wholesale prices. Contact us to place bulk or custom orders.

Fruits

Our fruits are handpicked from local orchards to ensure great taste and quality. We supply fresh seasonal fruits in bulk for events, functions, and hospitality needs.
